What is the difference between commercial and non-commercial?

Commercial accommodation includes hotels, hostels, or anywhere the student entered in to a formal agreement to pay for their stay.

Non-commercial accommodation usually does not include a formal contract or payment. Examples include:

  • staying with friends and family
  • renting a room in someone's house
  • accommodation provided by the hospital

Accommodation arranged through an online platform, such as Airbnb, can be either. If the students' booking entered them into a contract, it's commercial. If their booking was an informal agreement, it's non-commercial.
