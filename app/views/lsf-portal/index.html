Iteration 9

Travel and Dual Accommodation Expenses (TDAE) Version 3 Taxis and Overseas Costs Implementing a digital process for HEIs to approve or reject TDAE claims from students, including returning a claim to student and university returning a claim.

What we created:

  • TDAE Version 3 Taxis
  • TDAE Version 3 Overseas
  • Added the claim dates and value to each individual claim
  • New dashboard and central navigation
  • The ability to add additional comments when checking costs that require (pre)authorisation

What we plan to test:

  • TDAE Version 3 Taxis
  • TDAE Version 3 Overseas
  • New dashboard and central navigation
  • The ability to add additional comments when checking each section of the claim

What we found:

  • HEIs appreciate the context that is now able to be provided through the digital platform. For example, the reason why a student needed to book a taxi, who authorised the taxi/overseas costs, and any further comments from the student about these costs as they can be a bit trickier to handle.
  • Whilst it's more of an important feature for the NHSBSA Processors/CCS Agents, HEIs also appreciate being able to see the claim dates and values as sometimes they find students will refer to their claims using these two attributes when sending emails and such.
  • HEIs like the new centralised navigation system seen across the top navigation menu. They now find it easier to get from one section of the portal to another e.g., Student Search to
  • HEIs really like the ability to add additional comments when checking costs that require (pre)authorisation. HEIs have explained that a lot of students will write the bare minimum throughout their claim so having the ability to provide some extra context like any email correspondence between themselves and the student will be great for transparency between the student, HEI and NHSBSA.

Any known issues?

  • Not all evidence is representative of the associated cost - more to show functionality.

Iteration 8

Travel and Dual Accommodation Expenses (TDAE) Version 3 Hire Car scenario Implementing a digital process for HEIs to approve or reject TDAE claims from students, including returning a claim to student and university returning a claim.

What we created:

  • TDAE Version 3 Hire Car
  • TDAE Version 3 Placement mileage scenario also included

What we plan to test:

  • TDAE Version 3 Hire Car

What we found:

  • HEIs find hire cars easier to check with the new information that is provided. They like that the student can provide specific details around the hire car booking start and end date, who the booking was authorised by so that they can communicate further with this person if needs be, and the reason for the booking. HEIs also like that the student can provide further detail of their hire car booking/cost using a free text box to provide that extra bit of context if needs be.
  • HEIs really like that they can see the student's placement mileage journey via a Google Maps link as it speedens this checking element up.
  • HEIs also like that the system will show the Placement extra costs page even if it the student has not claimed for any extra costs during their journey. It just provides that extra piece of info whilst checking other parts of the claim.
  • Again, HEIs mentioned that they particularly like that the breakdown shows how the system has calculated the total costs to be reimbursed to the student with the university journey deducted as this provides extra piece of mind whilst checking.

Any known issues?

  • Not all evidence is representative of the associated cost - more to show functionality.

Iteration 7

Travel and Dual Accommodation Expenses (TDAE) Version 3 Implementing a digital process for HEIs to approve or reject TDAE claims from students, including returning a claim to student and university returning a claim.

What we created:

  • TDAE Version 3 Public Transport Placement Travel
  • TDAE Version 3 Accommodation Bookings
  • TDAE Version 3 Accommodation Travel journeys
  • The ability to add a feedback note to the student's claim and view the claim notes and history

What we plan to test:

  • TDAE Version 3 Public Transport Placement Travel
  • TDAE Version 3 Accommodation Bookings
  • TDAE Version 3 Accommodation Travel journeys
  • The ability to add a feedback note to the student's claim and view the claim notes and history

What we found:

  • HEIs appreciate the duplicate public transport ticket warnings with links to check the previous claim. They think this will reduce the back-and-forth element between the student, university and NHSBSA.
  • HEIs also like being able to view the breakdown of journey costs as it reduce their workload - A lot of the time, HEIs will double check student's calculation through the current paper PDF form, so the new system will take that element away.
  • HEIs really like the ability to add a feedback note to the student's claim and view the claim notes and history as it's useful to have that extra context and to see various comments left by other members of staff or the NHSBSA.

Any known issues?

  • Not all evidence is representative of the associated cost - more to show functionality.
