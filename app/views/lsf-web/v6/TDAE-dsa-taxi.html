Taxi costs details (for DSA students)

What are these costs?

If a student claiming Disabled Students' Allowance (DSA) took a taxi during their placement, DSA may have reimbursed some costs towards that.

They calculate what they reimburse using the actual cost of the journey minus what the public transport journey would have cost.

Students can claim back the public transport journey cost that DSA did not.
