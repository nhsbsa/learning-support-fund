What if your contribution covers more than one day?

If you pay for lifts over multiple days, such as a weekly payment, you'll need to calculate the daily cost.

You'll need to divide the amount you paid by the number of days you got a lift. 

For example

You got a lift from your friend 5 days a week. You paid them £10 per week. The daily cost is £2.
