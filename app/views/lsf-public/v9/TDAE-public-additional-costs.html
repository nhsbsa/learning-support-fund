What if my ticket covers more than one day?

If you purchase a pass, such as a weekly bus ticket, you will need to calculate the daily cost.

To do this, you need to divide the total cost you paid by the number of days your pass is valid for.

For example, you bought a weekly bus ticket for £14. The daily extra cost is £2 per day.
