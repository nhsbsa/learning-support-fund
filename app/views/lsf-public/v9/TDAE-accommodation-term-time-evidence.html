Upload evidence for term time accommodation costs

You'll need to add evidence of your term time accommodation costs which covers the dates of all your accommodation bookings.

If you have a mortgage on your property, upload the relevant evidence from your provider.

If you rent your property, upload your tenancy agreement or a letter from your landlord.

Uploading a tenancy agreement

The letter must be on headed paper if available and include their name, address, telephone number and email address.

  • name
  • address
  • telephone number
  • email address
Rules for evidence files

Evidence must be:

  • readable, not blurry
  • the right way up - not upside down or sideways
  • JPG, JPEG, PNG or PDF format
  • no more than 8MB
I'm using an iPhone or iPad

Any photos you take of your evidence may be in HEIC format. This is also known as HEIF. We cannot accept photos in this format.

To check if you need to change your Camera settings to make JPEG your default image format, visit Apple Support's advice (opens in a new tab) on this.
