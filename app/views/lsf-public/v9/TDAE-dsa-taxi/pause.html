If you took a taxi during your placement, Disabled Students' Allowance (DSA) may have reimbursed some of the costs for that.

They calculate what they pay you using the actual cost of your journey minus what the public transport journey would have cost.

You can claim back the public transport journey cost that DSA did not.

You'll need to give us:

  • evidence of the taxi cost
  • your DSA2 letter with the public transport equivalent cost on

If you used multiple taxis, you can add these after you have told us about the first one.
