Register for an NHS Learning Support Fund account

Easily manage your information by signing up for an NHS Learning Support Fund account

By registering an account, you can easily:

  • apply for NHS Learning Support Fund
  • update your personal details
  • check the status of your claims
  • view details of your scheduled payments including when your next payment will be made

In order to register for an account you will need:

  • an active email account
  • your postal address and contact information

Do not create an account if you are a final year student who may need to extend their course. Please visit our website for further information.

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Other ways to manage your account

You are in control of the personal information linked to your account. It is your responsibility to regularly check this as inaccurate information could result in delays to your payments.
