What type of accommodation is this?

The costs we reimburse you for are capped depending on your accommodation type. The maximum amounts we can reimburse are:

  • Up to £82.50 per night for commercial accommodation
  • Up to £37.50 per night for non-commercial accommodation
What is the difference between commercial and non-commercial?

Commercial accommodation includes hotels, hostels, or anywhere you enter in to a formal agreement to pay for your stay.

Non-commercial accommodation usually does not include a formal contract or payment. Examples include:

  • staying with friends and family
  • renting a room in someone's house
  • accommodation provided by the hospital

Accommodation arranged through an online platform, such as Airbnb, can be either. If your booking entered you into a contract, it's commercial. If your booking was an informal agreement, it's non-commercial.
