Overseas costs

You can claim back for overseas placements the cost of:

  • insurance
  • medical tests
  • vaccinations
  • visa fees

You can also claim back travel and accommodation within the UK or the host country. You can do this in the same way you would claim these costs within the UK.

You can not claim:

  • travel from the UK to the host country such as air fares
  • placement arrangement fees and any associated administrative fees
  • additional tuition fees charged by the placement provider
  • food
  • tips
  • phone calls
