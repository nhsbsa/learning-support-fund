Overseas costs

If your placement was outside the United Kingdom, the Isle of Man, or the Channel Islands, you can claim costs for:

  • insurance
  • medical tests
  • vaccinations
  • visa fees

If you paid for travel or accommodation in the UK and Islands or the host country, you should claimÂ these costs separately by selecting Change what costs you pay for.

You cannot claim for any other costs you paid for while overseas, such as:

  • travel from the UK or Islands to the host country, such as air fares
  • placement arrangement or administrative fees
  • additional tuition fees charged by the placement provider
  • food, tips, or general spending
  • phone call charges
