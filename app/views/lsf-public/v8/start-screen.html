Register for an NHS Learning Support Fund account

Once you've registered for an NHS Learning Support Fund account, you'll be able to:

  • apply for the NHS Learning Support Fund
  • update your personal details
  • check when your payments are due

Important:

If you're a final year student and you may be extending your course, do not create an account. Visit our website for more information on what you need to do (opens in a new tab).

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

We'll ask you for your:

  • email address
  • mobile number
  • date of birth
  • home address

We'll also ask for your National Insurance number, if you have one.

If you need help using the service

If you have any questions about registering for an NHS Learning Support Fund account, you can contact us (opens in a new tab).
