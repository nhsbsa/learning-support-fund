Information:

You can only claim taxi costs if you have authorisation from your university or placement. You'll need to upload evidence of this authorisation. For example, an email or note.

You need to tell us about any taxi journeys you paid for relating to your placement. For each one, we need to know:

  • who authorised you to use a taxi
  • the reason you used a taxi
  • the date you travelled
  • the cost you paid

You also need to show us evidence for the costs, such as receipts or invoices.

If you used multiple taxis, you can add these after you have told us about the first one.
