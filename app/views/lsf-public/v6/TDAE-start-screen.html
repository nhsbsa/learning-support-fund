Claim Travel and Dual Accommodation Expenses

When claiming online you need to tell us your university address, your placement addresses, and information about your travel and accommodation costs.

You will also need to give us evidence for the travel or accommodation costs you paid.

Examples of evidence you need to show us are:

  • parking charges
  • tunnel or toll charges
  • congestion or clean air charges
  • accommodation charges

You do not need to give us evidence of petrol costs.

Evidence can be images, screenshots, or PDFs of receipts, permits, or invoices.
