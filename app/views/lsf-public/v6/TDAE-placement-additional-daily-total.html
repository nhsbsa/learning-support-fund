What if your additional cost covers outside your claim dates?

If you purchase a pass for example a monthly parking ticket, you will need to provide this additional costs as a daily amount

Breaking down to daily

The total for a 1 month parking ticket to be divided by the amount of days you attended placement for the period you are claiming for.

For example:

£20 monthly parking pass divided by 10 days at placement, totals a daily additional cost of £2.
