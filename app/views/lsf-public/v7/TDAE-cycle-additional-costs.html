What if your extra cost covers more than one day?

If you purchase a pass, such as a monthly bicycle storage, you will need to calculate the daily cost.

Calculating the extra daily cost

You need to work out:

  • how many days you cycled to university in the period your pass covers
  • how many of those days are in the period you're claiming for

Then divide the total cost of your pass by the number of eligible days for the correct daily cost.

For example

You bought a monthly bicycle storage pass for £20, but only cycled to university on 10 days in the period you're claiming for. The extra daily cost is £2.
