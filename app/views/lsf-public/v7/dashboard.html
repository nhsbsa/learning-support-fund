Understanding your payments

This section splits your payments into the relevant terms for each academic year so you can view itemised amounts and a total for each term. Each term will offer the following information:

  • Type – NHS Learning Support Fund element description
  • Amount - the amount due to you
  • Expected date - this is the date we anticipate we will make payment based on your cohort and your attendance status being 'Matched student – in attendance'. This is only a predicted date and the actual date of payment is only confirmed once payment date is populated.

    Note: This will not display for Travel and Dual Accommodation Expenses and Exceptional Support Fund payments. These payment types are issued immediately once approved, so will only display a payment date once this is confirmed.

  • Payment date - this is the actual date a payment will credit your nominated bank account. This will only populate when the true payment date has been confirmed via our payment release process.

Understanding your overpayments

Where you have been overpaid an element of NHSLSF funding, we will add detail of this to the respective term. The award element(s) overpaid and amount(s) will be listed.

Overpayments will reduce the total amount payable in each term.

If we cannot recover any overpaid amount from future payments, they will be passed to our finance department for recovery via an alternative method. They will write to you to arrange this.
