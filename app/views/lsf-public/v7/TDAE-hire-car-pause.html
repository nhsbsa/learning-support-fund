Add your hire car details

You need to tell us about any cars you've hired to travel to or from your placements. For each one, we need to know:

  • the cost you paid
  • the start and end date of the booking
  • who from your university authorised the car hire
  • the reason why you hired a car

You also need to show us evidence for your car hire, such as receipts or invoices.

We only need details about your bookings, not the cars themselves. So you can enter a single booking with multiple cars together. However, multiple bookings for the same car need to be separated.

If you had multiple bookings, you can add these after you have told us about the first one.
