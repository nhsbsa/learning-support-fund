Upload evidence for this accommodation this booking

Evidence includes invoices or receipts. Invoices do not need to be business ones. All evidence must include your name, details of where you stayed, the costs you paid and the dates.

Evidence must be:

  • JPG, JPEG, PNG or PDF format
  • no more than 8MB

Before you upload your evidence, check that it's not upside or sideways. If it is, rotate it until it's readable.

I'm using an iPhone or iPad

Any photos you take of your evidence may be in HEIC format. This is also known as HEIF. We cannot accept photos in this format.

To check if you need to change your Camera settings to make JPEG your default image format, visit Apple Support's advice (opens in a new tab) on this.
