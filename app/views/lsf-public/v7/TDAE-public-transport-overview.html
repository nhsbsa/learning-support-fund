What am I reimbursed for a multiple day ticket?

We divide the cost of your ticket by the number of days it's valid for. We then multiply this by the number of days you used the ticket.

For example, if you bought a weekly ticket lasting 7 days for £7, the daily cost is £1. If you used this ticket on 5 days, then you will be reimbursed £5.

We will automatically calculate this for you.
