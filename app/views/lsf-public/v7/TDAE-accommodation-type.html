What type of accommodation is this?

The costs we reimburse you for are capped depending on your accommodation type. The maximum amounts we can reimburse are:

  • Up to £82.50 per night for commercial accommodation
  • Up to £37.50 per night for non-commercial accommodation
What is the difference between commercial and non-commercial?

Commercial accommodation requires an agreement where payments are enforceable by law. For example, hotels.

Non-commercial accommodation includes staying with friends and family or renting a room in someone's house. This usually does not include a formal contract or payment.

Accommodation advertised through an online platform, such as Airbnb, can be either. If your booking entered you into a contract, it is commercial. If your booking was an informal agreement, it is non-commercial.
