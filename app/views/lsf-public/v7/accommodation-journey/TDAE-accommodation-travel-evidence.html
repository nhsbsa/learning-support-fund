Evidence must be:

  • JPG, JPEG, PNG or PDF format

Before you upload your evidence, check that it's not upside down or sideways. If it is, rotate it until it's readable.

I'm using an iPhone or iPad

Any photos you take of your evidence may be in HEIC format. This is also known as HEIF. We cannot accept photos in this format.

To check if you need to change your Camera settings to make JPEG your default image format, visit Apple Support's advice (opens in a new tab) on this.

I do not have evidence

If you do not give us evidence for a cost, your university may ask you to remove it from your claim. Costs without evidence are only reimbursed in exceptional circumstances.

You can remove the journey from your claim or tell us why you cannot give us evidence.
